About Change Their Lives

Welcome to Change Their Lives, a non-profit organization committed to transforming lives through education and work opportunities. Our mission is to bridge the educational and skills gap, opening doors to advanced careers in the technology sector for underserved communities.

At Change Their Lives, we believe in learn-while-you-earn models to advance the US workforce, empower communities, and provide a better standard of living for more families. Our organization was born out of a desire to give people in need access to paid educational work opportunities.

We help provide paid apprenticeships to a diverse range of individuals, including Veterans, Military Spouses, At-Risk Youth, Non-Traditional Hires, the Unemployed or Underemployed, Employer Up-skill Programs, Single Parents, LGBTQ+, and more.
